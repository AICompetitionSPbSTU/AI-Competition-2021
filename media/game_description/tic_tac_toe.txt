Tic Tac Toe
Classic game, which everyone played at least one time in childhood.

What the rules?
You have square playing field 3x3 with cells. Player and bot move in turn. Player's move is 'X', bot's is 'O'. Player moves first always. To win you must fill 3 cells in succession in a row, in a column or diagonally.
