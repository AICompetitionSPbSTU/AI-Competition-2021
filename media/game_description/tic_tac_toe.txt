Tic Tac Toe is a classic game, which everyone played at least one time in childhood.
What the rules? You have a square playing field 3x3 with cells. Player and bot move in turn.
The Player's move is 'X', bot's is 'O'. The player moves first always.
To win you must fill 3 cells in succession in a row, in a column/ line or diagonally.
