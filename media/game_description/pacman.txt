Pacman is a fun Japanese game. What the rules? In this game player controls a yellow circle and bot controls a red circle.
Your goal is to collect as many coins as you can. The game ends as soon as all
the coins are collected and the one who collects the largest amount of coins wins.
Use the arrows: up, down, left, right to control your character.
